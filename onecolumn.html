Working For You

My name is Aseem Jha and I am the Republican candidate for West Lafayette City Council from the Third District. I believe that together, we can take advantage of this great opportunity for self governance. Purdue has already become something of a national sensation after the innovative measures being tried at the administrative level. By having a voice in the city government that truly represents you, that will stand up for your freedom, that will fight to ensure that every dollar is justly spent, we can show that our generation and our school is ready to step up, take the reins, and rework democracy to work for the people.
