Contact Us and Get Involved

All contact information is listed to the right

If you would like to get involved in the Jha Campaign, feel free to reach out. We are currently looking for passionate individuals to serve as Deputy Campaign Managers in the following residence halls: First Street Towers, Earhart Hall, Wiley Hall, Tarkington Hall, and Owen Hall. Individuals interested should contact Pierson Furnish, Head Campaign Manager or can alternatively reach out to me directly.

If you live in Hillenbrand, Shreve, Third Street, Meredith, Cary or Windsor and are interested in helping out at an Assistant Deputy Manager (i.e. as a campaign volunteer), then please reach out to your respective hall DM. Once again, all pertinent contact information is listed to the right.

If you just want to ask me about an issue, tell me about a problem, or suggest an initiative, I can be reached at any time. I will get back to you as soon as possible, however, I kindly request that all direct contact be limited to campaign and policy-related issues.

Pancake Thursdays

Every Thursday, at 10:00am on the dot, I head over to Hillenbrand Hall for some pancakes. Feel free to join me to discuss any policy related concerns or just chat about how classes are going. I'll be in the Sautter Hall (i.e. the big one in the back) so just pull up a chair and we can chat!

Donate to the cause

All donations should be made out to "Jha for City Council" and can be given in person to either Aseem or Isaac. If you would like to donate online, a PayPal portal will be set up shortly.

Working For You

My name is Aseem Jha and I am the Republican candidate for West Lafayette City Council from the Third District. I believe that together, we can take advantage of this great opportunity for self governance. Purdue has already become something of a national sensation after the innovative measures being tried at the administrative level. By having a voice in the city government that truly represents you, that will stand up for your freedom, that will fight to ensure that every dollar is justly spent, we can show that our generation and our school is ready to step up, take the reins, and rework democracy to work for the people.
