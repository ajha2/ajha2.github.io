Aseem Jha

Responsibility, Service, Transparency

My name is Aseem Jha, and I am currently a freshman studying Electrical Engineering. I am from Illinois originally, where my repeated exposure to the corruption and inefficiency of government in my own backyard drew me to public service. I am a problem solver by nature and I view government as a tool that can be made to work effectively for the people. Ultimately, I hope that my sense of pragmatism will help to bring the student body closer to the City of West Lafayette and to increase student involvement in civic matters. My goal is to resolve our problems in the most fiscally responsible way while still considering the needs of the greater community.
